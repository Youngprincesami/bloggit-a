Best Offline Games to Try in 2025

As gaming technology advances and developers push the boundaries of storytelling and gameplay, 2025 is shaping up to be an incredible year for offline games. Whether you're looking for epic adventures, challenging RPGs, or immersive worlds to explore without an internet connection, there are numerous highly anticipated titles to look forward to. In this guide, we'll take a look at some of the best offline games in 2025, each offering a unique and engaging single-player experience.


1. The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild 2

After the immense success of the original Breath of the Wild, the sequel promises to expand on the open-world formula that captivated millions of gamers. Breath of the Wild 2 takes everything players loved about its predecessor—vast landscapes, intricate puzzles, and deep exploration—and enhances it with new mechanics, environments, and even more mysteries to uncover. Set to offer fresh challenges and secrets, the game immerses players in a world where they can lose themselves for hours.

Key Features

  • Expansive open-world exploration with new environments and mechanics.
  • A deeply engaging storyline featuring new characters and factions.
  • Innovative puzzle-solving and combat mechanics that add more depth to gameplay.
  • Seamless integration of new weather systems, dungeons, and hidden areas to discover.

Why Play?

If you loved the freedom and exploration in the original Breath of the Wild, this sequel will elevate that experience. With even more secrets to discover, challenging puzzles, and an improved combat system, it’s sure to be a standout title for fans of fantasy adventure games.

2. Final Fantasy XVI

For RPG fans, Final Fantasy XVI is one of the most anticipated offline games of 2025. This installment in the legendary franchise builds on its predecessors with a darker and more mature story, set in a richly detailed world. Combining breathtaking visuals with an epic fantasy narrative and real-time combat, this game promises to deliver an unforgettable experience for players.

Key Features

  • An epic fantasy story filled with political intrigue, betrayal, and epic battles.
  • Real-time combat that offers greater fluidity and intensity, featuring dynamic magic and summons.
  • A richly detailed world with beautiful landscapes, cities, and dungeons to explore.
  • Engaging character arcs that explore themes of power, loss, and revenge.

Why Play?

If you're a fan of high-stakes RPGs with deep character development and captivating stories, Final Fantasy XVI is a must-play. The game’s refined combat system, rich world-building, and mature themes make it one of the most exciting RPG releases for 2025.

3. Elden Ring: Shadows of the Past

Following the success of Elden Ring, FromSoftware is bringing fans a standalone expansion titled Shadows of the Past. This new chapter will delve deeper into the iconic dark fantasy world that captivated players worldwide, offering new lore, characters, and even more challenging bosses. Expect a fresh layer of difficulty, exploration, and hidden secrets in this expansion.

Key Features

  • New lore and quests that explore untold stories from the Elden Ring universe.
  • Challenging bosses with unique attack patterns, requiring skill and strategy to defeat.
  • Expansive and atmospheric world-building that continues the dark fantasy narrative.
  • Increased difficulty, with new enemies and obstacles to overcome.

Why Play?

If you loved the challenge and intricacy of the original Elden Ring, this expansion will deepen your connection with its world. With new bosses, locations, and narrative arcs to uncover, it’s a perfect choice for fans who crave more of the game’s brutal and immersive experience.

4. Hogwarts Legacy

Step into the magical world of Harry Potter like never before with Hogwarts Legacy. This action RPG, set in the wizarding world, lets players experience life as a student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Set in the 1800s, long before the events of the Harry Potter books, the game offers players the chance to explore iconic locations, learn spells, and forge their own magical legacy.

Key Features

  • Customize your own student character, selecting their house, abilities, and magical specialization.
  • Explore iconic locations from the Harry Potter universe, including the Forbidden Forest, Diagon Alley, and more.
  • Engaging storyline with branching paths, allowing for different outcomes based on player choices.
  • Magical combat that combines spell-casting, potion-making, and exploration.

Why Play?

If you’ve ever dreamed of attending Hogwarts and living out your own magical adventures, Hogwarts Legacy provides the perfect opportunity to do just that. Whether you’re solving magical mysteries, battling dark wizards, or simply exploring the wizarding world, it’s an experience that Harry Potter fans won’t want to miss.

5. Star Wars Jedi: Survivor

Star Wars Jedi: Survivor is the highly anticipated sequel to the beloved Star Wars Jedi: Fallen Order, and it promises to deliver an even more thrilling adventure. As Cal Kestis, players will continue to navigate the perils of a galaxy in turmoil, facing new challenges, foes, and allies. With improved combat mechanics, a richer story, and deeper exploration, Jedi: Survivor is shaping up to be one of the top offline games for 2025.

Key Features

  • Improved combat mechanics, including lightsaber battles and Force powers.
  • A compelling, story-driven Star Wars experience with a focus on character growth and exploration.
  • New locations and iconic Star Wars planets to explore.
  • Challenging puzzles and intense battles with new and familiar enemies.

Why Play?

If you’re a Star Wars fan and enjoy action-packed adventures, Star Wars Jedi: Survivor is a must-play. With an expanded narrative, improved gameplay mechanics, and the opportunity to explore more of the Star Wars universe, it’s set to be an unforgettable experience for both new and returning players.

Conclusion: The Best Offline Games of 2025

2025 is looking to be an incredible year for offline gaming, with a variety of highly anticipated titles set to offer immersive, expansive, and exciting experiences for solo players. From the expansive worlds of Breath of the Wild 2 and Final Fantasy XVI to the dark fantasy challenges of Elden Ring: Shadows of the Past and the magical adventures of Hogwarts Legacy, there's something for every type of gamer. Whether you're seeking epic battles, deep lore, or the chance to explore enchanting realms, these offline games will provide hours of entertainment without the need for an internet connection.

Stay up to date on the latest details for these upcoming releases by checking trusted sources like GameSpot and IGN for news, trailers, and gameplay previews. Don't miss out on the best offline games of 2025!
