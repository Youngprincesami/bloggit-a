Here Are Five of the Best Multiplayer Mobile Shooter Games You Should Try in 2025

Multiplayer mobile shooter games have grown in popularity over the years, and 2025 looks set to continue this trend with some of the most thrilling and action-packed titles ever released. These games allow players to jump into fast-paced, competitive, and immersive experiences on their smartphones. Whether you’re looking for intense battle royale matches, hero-based gameplay, or a more tactical and strategic experience, there’s something for every shooter game enthusiast.

Mobile shooters not only offer a fun and fast way to game on the go but also deliver incredible visuals, challenging gameplay, and the opportunity to play against or team up with others from around the world. In this article, we’ve compiled a list of the top five multiplayer mobile shooter games you should definitely check out in 2025. Let’s dive in!


  1. Free Fire: Fast-Paced Battle Royale Fun

    • Genre: Battle Royale
    • Why Play: Free Fire is one of the most popular battle royale games on mobile devices, known for its quick, action-packed matches that last around 10 minutes. This game is perfect for players who don’t have hours to dedicate to a single game but still want the intense thrill of a battle royale experience. Free Fire is optimized for a wide range of mobile devices, meaning it runs smoothly on both high-end and lower-end smartphones, allowing more players to enjoy the game regardless of the device they own.
    • Highlights: Free Fire’s short match durations make it ideal for quick gaming sessions, whether you’re on the move or just looking for a quick adrenaline rush. Additionally, the game features a unique set of characters, each with their own special abilities. These abilities can add a layer of strategy to the game, making it even more engaging. The mobile-optimized gameplay ensures smooth performance on a wide range of devices, even for lower-end smartphones.
    • Why It Stands Out: The unique characters in Free Fire provide an extra level of customization, as each character’s abilities can complement different play styles. Additionally, the game’s accessibility on a variety of devices allows a wider audience to enjoy this exhilarating battle royale experience.

  2. Call of Duty Mobile (CODM): Iconic Shooter Action on the Go

    • Genre: First-Person Shooter / Battle Royale
    • Why Play: Call of Duty has been a staple in the first-person shooter (FPS) genre for years, and now, CODM brings the iconic series to mobile devices. Whether you’re a fan of team deathmatch, domination, or battle royale modes, CODM delivers a thrilling FPS experience with all the elements fans love. The mobile version retains the essence of the original franchise, offering maps, weapons, and game modes that will be familiar to longtime Call of Duty players.
    • Highlights: The game features stunning graphics that push mobile devices to their limits, ensuring that every explosion, gunshot, and firefight feels realistic. CODM offers various gameplay modes, including the classic Team Deathmatch, Search and Destroy, and a Battle Royale mode that has become a fan favorite. The controls are responsive and customizable, making it easy for players of all skill levels to jump in and enjoy.
    • Why It Stands Out: CODM stands out because it brings the legendary Call of Duty experience to mobile with an impressive level of detail. The game is packed with multiple modes, legendary maps from the console versions, and an active community that keeps the game fresh with regular updates.

  3. PUBG Mobile: Strategic and Realistic Battle Royale

    • Genre: Battle Royale
    • Why Play: PUBG Mobile is one of the earliest pioneers of the mobile battle royale genre. It’s known for its more tactical and slower-paced gameplay compared to other battle royale titles, offering a unique strategic experience. Whether you’re engaging in a 100-player showdown or playing the smaller squad-based modes, PUBG Mobile provides ample opportunities to plan your movements and outsmart your opponents. Frequent updates introduce new maps, weapons, and modes, keeping the game fresh for both veteran players and newcomers alike.
    • Highlights: PUBG Mobile’s large open-world maps offer plenty of room for exploration, and the game features realistic weapons and combat mechanics that make gunfights feel authentic. The variety of game modes, including classic 100-player battle royale, team-based squadrons, and arcade-style modes, ensure there’s always something new to try.
    • Why It Stands Out: PUBG Mobile stands out due to its commitment to offering a more realistic and tactical shooter experience. The game’s emphasis on strategy, combined with its realistic combat mechanics, makes it a favorite for players who prefer a slower, more methodical pace in their battle royale games.

  4. eFootball: Realistic Soccer Action with Multiplayer Thrills

    • Genre: Sports (Soccer/Football)
    • Why Play: eFootball, formerly known as Pro Evolution Soccer (PES), offers a highly realistic soccer gaming experience on mobile. It features licensed teams, real-life player models, and an immersive multiplayer experience where players can compete in online tournaments and matches. If you’re a soccer (football) fan who enjoys strategic gameplay, team-building, and competitive play, eFootball delivers all of that and more.
    • Highlights: eFootball’s attention to detail in terms of licensed teams, real-life player models, and dynamic football gameplay makes it one of the most realistic soccer games on mobile. The game also features frequent updates that reflect the real-world football season, introducing new events, player stats, and more.
    • Why It Stands Out: eFootball stands out because of its authenticity and realism. The game’s ability to replicate the thrill of real soccer matches and provide fans with a competitive multiplayer experience makes it a standout in the mobile sports genre.

  5. Apex Legends Mobile: Hero-Based Battle Royale

    • Genre: Battle Royale / Hero Shooter
    • Why Play: Apex Legends Mobile brings the highly acclaimed hero-based battle royale experience from the PC and console versions to mobile devices. Players take on the role of unique Legends, each with their own special abilities that can help turn the tide of battle. The mobile version retains the fast-paced action, thrilling gunplay, and team-based tactics that made the original a hit. If you’re looking for a shooter game that blends hero abilities with battle royale combat, Apex Legends Mobile is the perfect choice.
    • Highlights: Apex Legends Mobile features diverse characters with distinct abilities that encourage team-based strategies. Fast movement mechanics make combat intense and rewarding, while the immersive world design keeps players engaged. The game offers fast-paced, high-stakes battles that keep you on your toes.
    • Why It Stands Out: Apex Legends Mobile stands out by bringing a unique blend of hero-based abilities and battle royale gameplay. The game’s emphasis on teamwork, strategy, and intense action provides an exciting and dynamic experience that’s hard to match.

Conclusion: Dive Into These Thrilling Multiplayer Mobile Shooters

Whether you’re a fan of the tactical strategies found in PUBG Mobile, the fast-paced action of Free Fire, or the hero-based combat in Apex Legends Mobile, there’s a game for every type of shooter enthusiast. These multiplayer mobile shooters offer a wide variety of gameplay options, from large-scale battle royales to fast and furious team-based firefights.

2025 is shaping up to be an exciting year for mobile gaming, with these titles leading the charge in terms of innovation, gameplay depth, and competitive play. Each of the games we’ve discussed here offers something unique, so dive in and experience the next level of mobile shooter action!
